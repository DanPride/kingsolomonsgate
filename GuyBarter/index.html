Man arrested for exposing himself at Raymond Beach
By Ben Bragdon
Assistant Editor - Lakes Region Weekly
Email this story to a friend
Printer-friendly Format
Add your comments

RAYMOND (July 13, 2009): A Casco man was arrested July 12 after he allegedly exposed himself in the parking lot at the Raymond Beach.

Advertisement
[0]

Guy Barter, 57, was arrested at his home and charged with indecent conduct, a misdemeanor, after a witness provided to police a description of Barter and his vehicle, the Cumberland County Sheriff's Office said. Barter was taken to Cumberland County Jail and released on $45 bail.

Guy Barter (Image courtesy of Cumberland County Sheriff's Office)

The witness told police she saw a man pull into the parking lot wearing just a T-shirt and get out of his vehicle, police said. The man was later seen masturbating next to his vehicle while looking toward the beach. When the man noticed the witness, he got back into his vehicle and left.
