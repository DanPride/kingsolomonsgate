Sebago Land Trust invites it neighbors
and those friends of Byron G Pride still with us
for a Celebration of a Gift.

All Day Labor Day 2009
The Sebago Town Beaches

Thank You
Byron G Pride

    1. 8am - Noon

      Fry's Leap 5 Mile Swim

      Clean Side

      Contestants Swim from Fry's Leap to the clean side of the beach (a distance of 5 miles !). Swim starts at Frys leap at 8 am. All contestants must register with their adult boat partner. Each swimmer must pair with single watchboat. Winner is awarded annual ownership of the BG Pride cup for the Year.
      We are hopeful channel 6 will cover the race !

    2. 10am - Noon

      Classical Music Coffee and Tea

      Clean Side

      Classical music played on the mezzanine to the beach.
      Coffee, tea and cakes sponsored by the Sebago Chamber of Commerce Club

    3. 11am - Noon

      Toddler Spash Fest

      Clean Side
      Remnant

      A Cash $50.00 prise to the Mother of the Toddler who splashes the most and/or obviously has the most fun. If the mom is a single mon then the prize will be doubled to $100.00. Toddler warm-up period from 11 to 11:45. judging from 11.45 to Noon. Judge (Senator) Susan Collins presiding?
      Front Row seats provided for all Grandparents.

    4. High Noon

      Dedication - Awards - Raffle

      Clean Side
      Sandbar

      Judge (Senator) Susan Collins? will present the toddler splash-fest winner prize. Public comments by any with personal memories of Byron Pride. Remarks by childhood friend, WW2 B-17 pilot, and lifelong brother-in-law and friend, Arthur McCubrey. Comments by Chairman of Selectman and the Sebago Director of Public Parks.

    5. 1pm - 3pm

      Mark "Guitar" Miller

      Clean Side
      Mezzanine

      Folk and Rock music played by regional star Mark "Guitar" Miller and other local musicians on the mezzanine to the beach

    6. 1pm - 5pm

      Water Softball

      Boat Side
      Lakeside Beech

      Teams form up in the lower parking lot at 1 PM. All Welcome. Home plate is on the beech, the bases are buoy's. Hitting the runner with the ball on the skin (he has to be out of water) is an out.

    7. 1pm - 5pm

      Badminton on the Point

      Boat Side
      sandbar

      Badminton net on the sand bar. All Welcome.

    8. 1pm - 3pm

      Pig Roast and Barbeque

      Clean Side
      Mezzanine

      Full Pig fully roasted along with a selection of Burgers and Drinks.? Ice Cream by Richards Dairy Queen of Sebago?. Thai wok cooking by the Bangkok Restaurant, Portland Maine?

    9. 1pm - 5pm

      Water Volley Ball Tourney

      Clean Side
      Barrier

      Teams form up in the Clean Side Beech at 1 PM, All Welcome.
      Volleyball net will be in front of the stone barriers.

    10. 6pm - 8pm

      Jet Ski Barrel Racing

      Boat Side
      Lake Side

      Register in Advance. Adult Permission and Presence required for underage. Contestants will slalom a complex numbered buoy course under a stopwatch in contestant number order.
      Fastest time with no misses wins.

    11. Dusk-Dark

      Sparkler Ceremony

      Park Perimeter

      Everyone takes sparklers and lights them at dusk on the edges of the gift forming a large circle of sparklers accross the inlet.

    12. Dark-Closing

      Fireworks Display !

      Both Sides

      Fireworks by Atlas Fireworks.

      13. The Entire Park as delineated by the Gift will be Wreathed in Red Garland and Yellow roses for the Day.

      No parking allowed at the Clean Beach Entrance. Parking on the Boat side in the General Parking Area and Marina only.
      Shuttle bus service to the Clean Beech will be running from the Parking Lot for those who don't want to swim the channel.
      Channel Traffic cop will control All cross channel dingy, flotations and swimming for the day.

      All Proceeds go to the Sebago Fire Company
