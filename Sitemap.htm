My name is Dan Pride. I, with others, dug the hole,... with the sweat of my back,... that completed the first archaeological proof of a biblical passage in history. That's me in the picture (above right) from the A&E Documentary "Digging for the Truth" (Me in the Background, Yigael Yadin foreground), ...and ironically in the shows introduction of the title "Digging for the Truth" (above left).

I was an Allegheny College summer intern in archaeology in 1971 (Reference) and a member of the team who dug up King Solomon's Gate at Tel Gezer. The Solomonic Gate at Gezer was the third Solomonic Gate discovered. Combined with the discoveries of the Solomonic Gates at Megiddo in the 30's and Hazor in the 50's, The Gezer Gate completed the first proof of a biblical passage in history with rocks on the ground, ...passage 1 Kings 9:15.

We tackled the square with great enthusiasm and esprit de corp, each to the limits of our endurance, ...digging a 15x15 foot square 20+ feet deep in 120 degree heat and baking sunlight, in what was essentially a dirt moving operation (No netting back then ! ).
